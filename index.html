ControlsQuick Reference
Into the Crave: import the .seq into SynthTribe and store it there, or connect MIDI, choose a slot, and Send to Crave.
Tools: Select draws a marquee, Edit paints notes. Press B to switch tools. Drag the body to move, drag the right edge to extend, double-click to delete.
Badges: G=Glide, ×2/×3/×4=Ratchet, A=Accent.
Keys: ⌘/Ctrl+Z Undo · ⇧⌘Z/Ctrl+Y Redo · ⌘C/⌘X/⌘V copy/cut/paste · ⌫ delete · Arrow keys move · ⌘S saves the project.
